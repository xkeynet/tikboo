2. What cookies are
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by a website when you visit it.
Cookies may allow a website to recognize your browser, remember certain information, improve functionality, maintain security, measure performance, or provide a better user experience.
Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, scripts, and browser-based identifiers.

• To maintain session-related features where applicable.

• To measure traffic, performance, and technical behavior.
• To improve Tikboo, its layout, features, and user experience.
• To prevent abuse, fraud, automated access, or misuse of the service.
